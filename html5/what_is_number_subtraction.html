What is number subtraction?

Here you will learn what is subtraction.

To perform subtraction using the JPS Calculator app, it is essential to understand the concept of subtraction. Subtraction is a mathematical operation that allows you to find the difference between two numbers.

In the app, the subtraction operation is represented by the minus (-) button. When you subtract, you take one number away from another, resulting in a smaller number. This process is often described as reducing a value.

By mastering subtraction, you can effectively calculate differences and manage numerical operations within the app.
